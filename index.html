dekuwec

A family of environmentalists Treasuring environmental conservation and protecting the wildlife is our number one priority.

Water, trees, air, biodiversity and nature are the immutable guarantors of all our lives. If we do not protect, respect and value them, we are to be blamed.

LIFE IS BEAUTIFULL AND IT'S OUR RESPONSIBILITY!

You can not protect the environment unless you empower people, you inform them, and you help them understand that these resources are their own, that they MUST protect them.

mission

To influence, encourage and assist societies in conserving the integrity and diversity of nature and to protect and restore the environmental quality of sustainability.

vision

A world where we live sustainably in a healthy. environment that is valued and protected by all.

wildlife spotlight

llama

The Dedan Kimathi Conservancy hosts Andean llamas
Llamas are social animals with protective instincts, often serving as guard animals for livestock like sheep and goats. They form strong bonds with their herds, using their alertness and vocalizations to warn of potential threats, earning them the nickname "guard llamas."
